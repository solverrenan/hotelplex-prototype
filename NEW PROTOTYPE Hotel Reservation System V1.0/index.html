About us

Hotelplex is a leading provider of hotel accommodation, offering booking services through its own network of localised websites and telephone call centres.

Hotelplex gives travellers one of the widest selections of accommodation on the net, including both independent and major chain hotels as well as self-catering in over hundreds of thousands properties. The company offers a one-stop shopping source for hotel pricing, amenities and availability and also specialises in providing travellers with accommodation during sold-out periods.
